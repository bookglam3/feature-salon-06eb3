Brand Rules

✓ DO

  • · Use on dark or white backgrounds only
  • · Maintain minimum clear space (= icon height × 0.5)
  • · Use SVG for all digital applications
  • · Keep proportions locked — never stretch
  • · Use monochrome version for embossing / print
  • · Use favicon.svg for browser tab icons

✗ DON'T

  • · Place on busy or coloured backgrounds
  • · Add drop shadows or outer glow effects
  • · Use old raster (PNG/JPG) for large formats
  • · Change the typeface or alter letterforms
  • · Use the icon mark alone without approval
  • · Recreate the logo in other software
